Cribbage Trainer

discard practice or play vs the computer

Four ways to play. Sharpen your throw in the trainer, then put it to work against the computer — heads-up, 3-handed, or 4-player cutthroat.

♣
Discard Trainer
analyze & rank every throw
›
Deal a hand and see all discards ranked by expected points — hand value over every cut, crib swing, and pegging potential, each fully explained. Supports 4-, 3-, and 2-handed.
♥
Play a Game (vs 3 AI)
4-player cutthroat · first to 121
›
A complete game of 4-player cutthroat cribbage against three computer players: deal, discard, cut, interactive pegging, the show, and the race to 121. Auto-count or muggins.
♦
Three-Handed (vs 2 AI)
3-player cutthroat · first to 121
›
Three-player cutthroat cribbage against two computer players: everyone throws one to the crib and the dealer tops it up with a card off the deck, then cut, interactive pegging, the show, and the race to 121.
♠
Heads-Up (vs 1 AI)
2-handed · first to 121
›
Classic two-player cribbage against the computer: deal six, throw two to the crib, then cut, peg, and count your way to 121.
